An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing workforce data across departments, designations, salary structures, and hiring trends to help organizations make smarter HR decisions.

## 📂 Dataset Overview
**File:** `Employee_Insight_Data_Set.xlsx`

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Employee ID | Unique identifier for each employee |
| Department | Department the employee belongs to |
| Employee Designation | Job title / role |
| Year of Joining | Year the employee joined |
| Salary Before Tax (INR) | Gross salary in Indian Rupees |
| Income Tax (INR) | Tax deducted from salary |
| Salary After Tax (INR) | Net take-home salary |

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights
- 🏭 **Department Distribution** — Manufacturing leads with 29.66% of total headcount (43 employees)
- 💼 **Tax by Designation** — Senior Associates pay the highest average income tax (₹4,52,000)
- 📅 **Hiring Trends** — Peak hiring in 2006 & 2007 with 10 employees each year
- 💵 **Top Earner** — Employee Ma_00029 leads with a pre-tax salary of ₹83.34 Lakh
- 🔍 **Interactive Filters** — Slice data by Employee Designation and Year of Joining
